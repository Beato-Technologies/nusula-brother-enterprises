Business Profile
Nusula and Brothers Enterprises, Trading as “Fastbakes” is both a Trading and Manufacturing company specializing in Food ingredients. Since its establishment in 2018, the company is widely recognized as one of the most professional leading manufacturers and trader in food ingredients with a team of directors and senior executives who are experts in their chosen fields. The company initially started as a trading business which later transformed largely into manufacturing through research and innovation. The company Brand name “Fastbakes” has become a prominent brand in Uganda and East Africa. The company Headquarters offices are in Uganda. However, it has strategically expanded its operations in Tanzania in 2025 with the founding of Nusula and Brothers Company Limited in Dar es Salaam.

Our business has grown despite the various challenges. We are proud to say that we have created a strong bond with our clients. In the course of over 8 years, we have become an established caterer. In the coming decades, we will continue to transform our Industry by staying true to our core values.

MANAGEMENT
The company management board is made of 3 executives and 2 non-executive members with equal voting power. The Board is responsible for all regulatory functions relating to the company affairs, including strategic planning budgeting, goals, policies and overall oversight. However, the daily operations have been delegated to a number of executive directors, technical directors and executive managers.
